Onigiri

  1. 400 grams of rice
  2. Half a fish salmon (50 grams)
  3. Salt
  4. White sesame
  5. Algae
  • To prepare onigiri with salmon,we mix the sliced salmon with salt and let it rest for at least thirty minutes.
    Dry it and grill it in a pan.
    Carefully remove the skin and bones of the and fill its meat in pieces so-called feathers.
  • Put the rice in a bowl and make a hole in the middle to open a quarter of the cooked andstuffed salmon in the middle.
    Moisten your hands and cover with salt.
    Thake the brass ball in your hands and press it to form a ball.

Dumpling

  1. Round dough Eghty number
  2. Veal 500 Grams
  3. Large clove of garlic 4 number
  4. 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  5. Onion 140 grams
  6. 1 tablespoon of salt
  • First we prepare the meat. to do this put the frying pan on the gas beforehand and put the flame up to a medium high.
    Pour some frying oli and then fry the mince for one or two minutes.
  • Take a spoonfull of the filling and place it in the center of the dough.
    Pull the dough over the filling and press the wet edges to stick together.
  • Now put the mandoos in the freezer for four to five hours until.
    They are frozen and then put them in a zippered freezer bag and put them in the freezer.

Dango

  1. 100 grams og rice flour
  2. Half a cup of water
  3. 2 tablespoons of powdered suger
  4. 3 tablespoons of starch
  5. Pink and green food coloring
  • Mix rice flour and wheat flour together.
    Add water little by little stir until it becomes a conpletely uniform dough and you can hold it in your hand.
  • Grind the suger into powder and add it to the doughand mix.
  • Take some of the dough and make a small ball with each one.
    Now pour the dough into a pot of boiling water and let it boil for 3 to 5 minutes.
  • You should pass a wooden skewer through the dongos before they lose heat
    And if a wooden skewer is not available, you can put them on a plate
    And pour the sauce and enjoy!
